# Human Prediction Survey – Draft

## 1. Instructions for Participants
For each movie listed below:
- Read the provided information (English title, year, country, short plot summary)
- Predict the movie’s Douban rating on a **1–5 scale, one decimal**
- Rate your confidence in your prediction (1–5)

## 2. Questions for Each Movie
For each movie:
1. **Predicted Rating (1–5, one decimal):**  
What do you think this movie’s Douban rating is?
2. **Confidence Level (1–5):**  
How confident are you in your prediction?
3. **Have you watched this movie? (Yes/No)**

## 3. Movies included in the Survey
- Successor (2024)
- G for Gap (2024)
- Johnny Keep Walking! (2023)
- Twilight of the Warriors: Walled In (2024)
- Fly Me to the Moon (2024)
- 18x2 Beyond Youthful Days (2024)
- Evacuate from the 21st Century (2024)
- Wonka (2023)
- Look Back (2024)
- What Goes Around Comes Around (2024)
- Picnic (2023)

## 4. Optional Notes
Participants should answer based only on their intuition or prior experience.  
They should **not search** for ratings or reviews.
